Genocide’s Legacy: A Museum in a Khmer Rouge Prison

Spring 2017, I took Jason Maloney's GlobalBeat video course at NYU, which Jason builds each year around an international reporting trip. Our class traveled to Cambodia and collaboratively reported out several different stories. This one, for the New York Times, is a 360 (i.e., click and drag) video documenting how Cambodia memorializes the millions who died during Pol Pot's genocidal regime.
