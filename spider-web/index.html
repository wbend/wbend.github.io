Spider Web - The Making and Unmaking of Iran Sanctions

Built with the d3.js JavaScript library, this project illustrates the findings of the Crisis Group report of the same name. The graphic identifies the major unilateral, multilateral and international sanctions imposed on Iran and breaks each down by year, economic impact, trigger, targets, and reversibility.
