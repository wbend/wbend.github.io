The Central Asia Military Units Map is an interactive tool developed by the Oxus Society for Central Asian Affairs. It provides a comprehensive overview of military installations and units across the five Central Asian countries: Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan. This map offers valuable insights into the military landscape of the region, including the types and locations of various military facilities such as bases, airfields, and training grounds.

For inquiries about the Central Asia Military Units Map, please email info@oxussociety.org.
