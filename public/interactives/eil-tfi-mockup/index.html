In July 2016, TFI will partner with The Experiment in International Living to provide 120 Fellows with an experience centered on Cultural Exchange and Leadership Development in in South Africa. The Fellows will travel for approximately 16 days to South Africa to gain an experience that will provide a deep understanding of the cultural, social and political history. Fellows will have positive and meaningful interactions and dialogues with youth and leaders from various sectors of society such as: government, arts/music, business, education. Fellows will also participate in leadership and skill building opportunities. Finally, Fellows will visit and explore important historical and cultural sites.
